Jumpthrow Bind

Paste to console/add to autoconfig

alias "+jumpthrow" "+jump;-attack"
alias "-jumpthrow" "-jump"
bind "F1" "+jumpthrow"

Info!

Paste into console 1 line at a time OR add all 3 lines to autoexec. Replace the F1 Key with key of choice. Aim grenade, press and hold mouse 1, press the jumpthrow key. The grenade will be thrown at perfect peak of jump, 100% of the time.
