<h3><b>› Tiny Blogger Template</b></h3>
<img src="imgs/pen.gif" width="40" height="32" align="left" alt="pen.gif" />  I've been busying making templates these days, if you can't tell! And my next, "Tiny Blogger" has been one of my favorites to make. Tiny Blogger is beyound easy to use, and like all my templates cross-browser and W3CValid (XHTML). As well it only runs on 9.0kb of graphics for the layout. Most every one will find this template extremely easy to use, one look <a href="source.html">at the source</a> and you'll understand.
